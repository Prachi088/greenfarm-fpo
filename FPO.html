FPO Services and Benefits

  • Bulk Buying and Selling

    FPOs help farmers to collectively purchase inputs at lower costs and sell their produce in bulk, ensuring better prices and reducing transaction costs.

  • Government Schemes

    FPOs provide access to various government schemes and subsidies, helping farmers to benefit from financial support and development programs.

  • Training and Workshops

    FPOs organize training sessions and workshops to educate farmers on best practices, new technologies, and sustainable farming methods.

  • Loans and Financial Support

    FPOs facilitate access to loans and financial services, enabling farmers to invest in their farms and improve productivity.

  • Market Linkage Support

    FPOs connect farmers with markets, helping them to find better buyers and secure fair prices for their produce.

  • Technology and Innovation Adoption

    FPOs promote the adoption of new technologies and innovations in farming, enhancing efficiency and crop yield.
